What Is Lumivox Studios

Lumivox Studios is a creative technology studio founded and run end-to-end by Sufiyan Absar. There are no account managers and no hand-offs between teams — every project moves from first sketch to production deploy under one continuous line of thought, which is why the work ships fast and stays coherent.

From flagship websites to AI tooling and automation systems, Lumivox treats every build as a complete product, not a deliverable — designed, engineered, and shipped with the same standard the world's best technology brands hold themselves to.
